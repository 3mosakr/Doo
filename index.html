Types of Animals:

There are different types of animals live within the world, for instance, amphibians, reptiles, mammals, birds, and insects.

Amphibians

Those animals residing within each land and water and their skins are terribly sleek and wet and no scales on their body. That animal referred to as amphibians. There is some example of amphibians like a frog and also the toad.

Reptiles

Those animals would like daylight as a result of their cold blood and broken body for an extended time. That animal is named the reptiles. Such an example of the reptilian animals is snake and crocodilian reptile.

Mammals

Those animal have the nice and cozy blood, and furry body and that they would like the breastfeeds to be young. That animal is named the mammals. There is such an example of mammal’s animal-like Cow, Lion.

Birds

Those animals have wings for fly, beaks. That animal is that the birds. There are 2 models of the bird is Parrot and Crow.

Insects

The insects are small, and also the body of insects has 3 components like head, the thorax, and also the abdomen. it’s six legs, however no backbone. The insect can even fly sort of a bird. Bees and mosquitos referred to as insects.
